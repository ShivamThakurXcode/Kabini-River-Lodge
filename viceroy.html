Discover the Viceroy Experience at Kabini River Lodge

Welcome to Kabini River Lodge! Our Viceroy Package offers a comfortable and memorable stay amidst the serene beauty of Nagarhole. Nestled in the heart of Kabini, this package invites you to experience wildlife and wilderness up close while enjoying the comfort of a spacious bungalow room. Designed as a cozy retreat after a day full of adventure, the Viceroy Package allows you to immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of Kabini without compromising on comfort.

Unwind in Nature's Embrace

Wake up to the calls of native birds and venture into the lush landscapes where elephants and tigers roam freely. This package is ideal for those seeking relaxation and adventure, offering river safaris, nature walks, and jeep excursions with seasoned naturalists. Step into nature's embrace, discover Kabini's treasures, and let our team curate your perfect wilderness experience.

Things To Do
  • Irpu Falls, which is also known as Lakshmana Tirtha Falls, in the Brahmagiri Mountain Range, is only 37 kilometers away.
  • Cruise through the coffee estates of Kutta a mere distance of about 7 kilometers from the lodge.
  • Pay your respects at the ancient Rameshwara Temple made of stone. It is pretty close to Lakshmana Tirtha River.
  • Explore the different attractions of Madikeri like the Abbey Falls and Raja’s Seat. All it takes is a 2-hour drive.
  • Tibetan monastery in Kushalnagar about 90 kilometers away is a must-see place. It also gives you an avenue for shopping.
  • Spend an evening filled with fun and games at Kaveri Nisargadhama in Kushalnagar. It will take you a slightly longer than 2-hour drive.
  • Harangi Dam in Kushalnagar is another popular picnic spot.
  • Dubare elephant camp is located 75 km from Nagarahole.
