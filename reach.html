Your Journey to Kabini River Resort

Scenic routes, travel tips, and Everything you need to arrive relaxed.

Arriving at Kabini River Resort signals the start of your adventure. Whether driving through Karnataka's landscapes, flying from Mysore, or taking a scenic route from Bengaluru, this secluded lodge along the Kabini River near Nagarhole National Park promises an unforgettable experience in nature.

Mysore Airport to Kabini

Flying into Mysore Airport (MYQ) to reach Kabini River Lodge ? Here’s how to make the journey smooth and enjoyable.

Mysore Airport to Kabini
  • Mysore Airport to Kabini
  • Approx 1 hour 40 min

The simplest and quickest way to reach Kabini from Mysore Airport is by car or taxi. The lodge is around 75 kilometers away, and the drive takes about 1 hour and 40 minutes, depending on traffic.

You can book a taxi at the airport or arrange a rental if you prefer more freedom. Follow SH-33 towards H.D. Kote, and watch for signs pointing to Kabini or Jungle Lodges & Resorts to guide you directly to the lodge.

Travel Route
  • Start from Mysore Airport
  • Mysore Airport -> Hampapura
  • Handpost -> Antharsanthe
  • Malalagadde -> N. Belthur
  • Final Stop: Kabini River Lodge
This route takes you through several scenic towns and villages, making it a peaceful way to kick off your nature adventure.

Bengaluru Airport to Kabini

Flying into Kempegowda Airport (BLR) in Bengaluru ?Choose from a few scenic routes to reach the lodge.

Bengaluru Airport to Kabini
  • Kempegowda Airport to Kabini
  • Approx 6 hour 12 min

Driving is your best bet if you're looking for flexibility and comfort. Kabini River Lodge is about 260 kilometers from the airport, and the drive takes around 5 to 6 hours, depending on traffic.

With this option, you can set your own pace, stopping for snacks, photo ops, or a quick stretch as you pass through picturesque towns like Ramanagara, Mandya, and Mysore.

Travel Route
  • Bengaluru to Mysore
  • Jayapura -> Hampapura
  • Handpost -> Antharsanthe
  • Malalagadde -> N.Belthur
  • Final Stop: Kabini River Lodge
The route offers scenic views of lush fields and local markets. Taxi fares range from ₹5,500 to ₹7,000.
Bengaluru Airport to Kabini
  • Bengaluru -> Mysore -> Kabini
  • Approx 8 hour 00 min

Are you looking to save a bit? While not the fastest, taking a bus from Bengaluru can add a touch of adventure to your journey. KSRTC (Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation) runs buses from Bengaluru to Mysore.

For the final leg of the trip to Kabini, you'll switch to a local bus or taxi. The trip might take around 7 to 8 hours total, making it ideal if you're looking to soak up the local sights and sounds at a leisurely pace.

Travel Route
  • Bengaluru to Mysore
  • Jayapura -> Hampapura
  • Handpost -> Antharsanthe
  • Malalagadde -> N.Belthur
  • Final Stop: Kabini River Lodge
Bus fares from Bengaluru to Mysore range from ₹300–₹400, while a Mysore-to-Kabini transfer costs an additional ₹125 to ₹300.
Bengaluru Airport to Kabini
  • Bengaluru -> Mysore -> Kabini
  • Approx 5 hour 00 min

Consider the train for a balanced mix of comfort and budget-friendliness. Catch a train from Bengaluru City Railway Station to Mysore for a scenic 3-hour journey through Karnataka's countryside.

Once in Mysore, grab a taxi for the final 80 kilometers to Kabini, which takes about 2 hours and typically costs ₹2,500 to ₹4,000, depending on the taxi service.

Travel Route
  • Start from Bengaluru
  • Bengaluru Railway Station
  • Mysore Railway Station
  • Final Stop: Mysore. -> Kabini Lodge
This option offers the comfort of the train and a scenic road trip from Mysore to Kabini, giving you the best of both worlds.

Whichever route you choose.

The journey is part of the adventure. With green fields, quiet towns, and winding roads, the drive sets the perfect tone for your stay at Kabini River Lodge, where you'll soon be surrounded by nature and wildlife.
