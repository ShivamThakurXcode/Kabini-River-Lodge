Travel Directions
  • Start from Bengaluru City Bus Station
  • Take the direct bus towards Kabini (Buses available from 6 AM to 8 PM)
  • Scenic Route via Mysore - Kabini Road
  • Total Journey Time: 5-6 hours
  • Drop-off: Kabini Bus Stop (10 mins from the lodge)
