Things To Do

Discover a range of memorable experiences near our lodge, from serene waterfalls to vibrant cultural sites. Plan your perfect day trips with these must-visit destinations just a short drive away.

  • Visit the serene Irpu Falls, also known as Lakshmana Tirtha Falls, set against the lush Brahmagiri Mountain Range. Only a 37-kilometer drive away, bring your camera to capture the falls and enjoy a scenic day trip.
  • Take a peaceful drive through Kutta’s coffee estates, only 7 kilometers from the lodge. The coffee fields make for an ideal quick outing and offer great spots to stop for fresh air and photos.
  • Experience the timeless beauty of the ancient Rameshwara Temple near the Lakshmana Tirtha River. It’s a beautiful place for quiet reflection and cultural discovery.
  • Discover Madikeri’s gems, including the famous Abbey Falls and Raja’s Seat, just a scenic 2-hour drive from the lodge. Enjoy the local sights and make the most of your day exploring.
  • Make a memorable stop at the Tibetan Monastery in Kushalnagar, around 90 kilometers away. Here, you can admire the peaceful surroundings, learn about Tibetan culture, and browse unique items at nearby shops.
  • Have a fun-filled evening at Kaveri Nisargadhama in Kushalnagar, a nature getaway worth the little over two-hour drive. Ideal for families, it offers activities for everyone to enjoy.
  • Plan a relaxed afternoon picnic at the famous Harangi Dam in Kushalnagar. The scenic views and peaceful surroundings make it an excellent spot for unwinding.
  • Visit the Dubare Elephant Camp, located 75 kilometers from Nagarahole, where you can see elephants up close, learn about their care, and even interact with these gentle giants.

Please Note

When planning your stay with us, here's what you need to know about our rates and services

  • Room Rates: Our rates are calculated on a per-person, per-night basis on twin sharing. If you prefer a private room, an additional 30% single occupancy charge will be added to the nightly rate.
  • Children's Rates: We warmly welcome young adventurers! Children aged 5 to 12 who stay with their parents enjoy a 50% discount on the rate.
  • Transfers: Please note that transportation to and from our resort is not included. However, we are happy to assist in arranging convenient options to ensure a smooth arrival.
  • Photography Fees: A separate entry fee for guests bringing cameras or additional lenses may apply.
  • Tariffs may be subject to change without prior notice.

We are committed to making your stay seamless, enjoyable, and unforgettable. If you have any questions or need assistance customizing your stay, please get in touch with us anytime! We look forward to welcoming you soon.
