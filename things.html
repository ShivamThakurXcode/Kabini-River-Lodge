Things To Do at Kabini River Lodge

Discover Unforgettable

Experiences at Kabini River Lodge

Situated on the edge of Nagarhole Tiger Reserve, Kabini River Lodge offers countless opportunities to immerse yourself in nature, unwind, and explore local history. Whether you want to spot a tiger or relax by the river, this guide will help you make the most of your stay.

Morning and

Evening Safaris

Experience the thrill of Nagarhole Tiger Reserve with two daily safaris—one at dawn and another in the evening. With trained naturalists leading the way, you'll explore habitats known for tigers, leopards, and elephants.

Each time of day brings unique sights and sounds, from deer gathering at waterholes to fresh tiger paw prints shimmering in the early morning light.

Boat Safari on

the Kabini River

The Kabini River is not just tranquil; it's alive with wildlife. Enjoy a boat safari as you cruise along the river, observing elephants, otters, and various bird species as they come to drink. In winter, the river becomes prime territory for spotting crocodiles and large flocks of migratory birds.

Birdwatching and

Nature Walks

Kabini is a top birdwatching spot in India because of its varied habitats. You can join guided nature walks and hike along trails to see some 250 bird species living in Kabini. Each step offers a chance to discover something new, from the bright Malabar trogon to the impressive crested hawk eagle.

Campfire Evenings

with Storytelling

After a day of exploration, unwind by the campfire, where guides share their life stories in the wild. Gather under a starlit sky, exchange sightings with other guests, and listen to locals recount the history and folklore of the Kabini region.

Photography

Expeditions

Kabini's lush forests, open grasslands, and river vistas create an ideal setting for photographers. Capture breathtaking moments, like the silhouette of a peacock at dawn or the intense gaze of a tiger from a distance. With tips from experienced guides, you'll take home photos that relive your adventure.

Sunset by

The River

Conclude your day at Kabini with a quiet moment by the river. Watch the sky transition from blue to amber as the sun sets over the water. This peaceful time offers a perfect reflection on a day filled with adventure and discovery, highlighting the beauty of simplicity.

Village Tour and

Cultural Experience

Venture beyond the lodge to visit nearby tribal villages, where traditional lifestyles remain strong. Learn about local customs, handicrafts, and the communities' connection with the forest. This cultural experience enriches your understanding of Kabini's human side. (Not included in the package)

Visit the

Tribal Museum

Explore the local tribal museum near the lodge, which provides insights into the indigenous cultures that have coexisted with Kabini's forests for centuries. From artifacts and tools to traditional life stories, it's a fascinating glimpse into a way of life shaped by the wilderness. (Not included in the package)

Evening Wildlife

Film Showings

If you want to learn more about the area's animals, join us for our evening wildlife film showings. You'll hear the fantastic stories of the animals that call Kabini home and learn about the vital work being done to protect them. It's a great way to get ready for your upcoming safari adventure!

Every experience here lets you dive into the stunning beauty of Kabini, explore its fascinating history, and see a variety of wildlife. Get ready to make unforgettable memories in nature. The Kabini River Lodge is prepared to welcome you!

Places to Visit Near Kabini River Lodge :

  • Irpu Falls: Just 37 km away, discover the stunning Irpu Falls, also known as Lakshmana Tirtha Falls, nestled in the Brahmagiri Mountain Range.
  • Coffee Estate Cruise: Journey through lush coffee plantations in Kutta, only 7 km from the lodge.
  • Rameshwara Temple: For a serene cultural experience, visit this ancient stone temple close to the Lakshmana Tirtha River.
  • Madikeri Sights: A 2-hour drive brings you to Madikeri’s Abbey Falls and the scenic Raja’s Seat.
  • Kushalnagar Monastery: This Tibetan monastery, 90 km away, offers cultural insights and unique shopping.
  • Kaveri Nisargadhama: This nature island is about two hours away and offers an evening of fun and games.
  • Harangi Dam: This famous picnic spot in Kushalnagar offers peaceful riverside views.
  • Dubare Elephant Camp: Located 75 km from Nagarhole, experience a hands-on encounter with elephants.

Each spot around Kabini offers something unique, from serene temples and coffee estate rides to vibrant Tibetan culture and wildlife experiences.
